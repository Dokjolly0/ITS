Simple omelette recipe

Preparation time

  • Total : approximately 10 minute
  • Preparation : approximately 5 minute
  • Cooking : approximately 5 minute

ingrediets

  • 2/3 large eggs
  • Salt: to taste
  • Pepper: to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

Istruction

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pink of salt and pepper until well mixed, you can add a tablestoon of water or milk for a flutter texture.
  2. Heat of pan: Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter has melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tit the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (opzional): When the eggs begin to set the edges but are stil slightly runny in the midle, sprinkle your choise fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold or save: As the omelette coninues to cook, carefuly lift one edge and fold it over the fillings, let it cook for another minute, then slide into a plate.
  6. Enjoi: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
